A table with some common and useful Angular Pipes. There are even custom ones

Variable Pipe description Product
{{name}} uppercase Turns the text to uppercase {{name | uppercase}}

{{name}} slice:3 Slices the text in the part said ( in this case 3) {{name | slice:3}}
{{name}} slice:0:3 Slices the text in the part said ( in this from 0 to 3) {{name | slice:0:3}}
{{array}} slice:1:5 Also slices arrays ( in this from 1 to 5) {{array | slice:1:5}}

{{salary}} currency:'EUR':'symbol-narrow':'4.0-0' formatting to euros without decimals {{salary | currency:'EUR':'symbol-narrow':'4.0-0'}}
{{promiseValue}} async to format value that come from promise or async {{promiseValue |async}}
